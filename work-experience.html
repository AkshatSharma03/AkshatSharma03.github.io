03 · Research in progress

Current research writing, including a co-authored manuscript in development and a journal manuscript submitted for peer review. These items are not presented as peer-reviewed publications.

Co-authored manuscript · In development

The Social Value of a Topic: A Value-of-Information Framework for Scoring Civic Messaging and Journalism

Theodore T. Allen, James Paskett, Yang Ching Wu, Long Wang & Akshat Sharma

Develops a decision-analytic framework for comparing candidate civic topics and messages by expected population-level public benefit, giving organizations a second decision signal alongside predicted engagement.

Separates message design from targeting; models reach, exposure, persuasion, evidence-aligned decision change, risk, reactance, and measurability; and treats language models as reviewable instruments while deterministic computation produces the score.

Journal manuscript · Submitted to Policy & Internet

Beyond Voluntary Artificial Intelligence Governance: Mandatory Liability Insurance for AI

Sharma, A.

Proposes mandatory liability insurance as a financial-responsibility framework for AI development and high-impact deployment, connecting legal accountability with market-based incentives for safer systems.

Develops risk triggers, meaningful-control allocation, underwriting safeguards, captive-insurance restrictions, exclusion safeguards, and a phased public backstop for risks that private markets cannot initially absorb.
