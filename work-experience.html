03 · Publications

Publications & working papers

Methods manuscript

A Neutral Survey Instrument for Modeling Safe-Sleep Message Effects: ENACT-R Measurement, Social Value Scoring, and Persuasiveness Validation

Sharma, A.

Introduces ENACT-R, an ACT-R-derived cognitive-behavioral model linking activation, source credibility, semantic cueing, affect, barriers, self-efficacy, recall, and habit to observed behavior.

Develops a neutral, parameter-mapped survey and randomized follow-up design that bridges message-level behavioral lift to population-level Social Value Scores while keeping assumptions auditable.

Working paper

Beyond Voluntary Artificial Intelligence Governance: Mandatory Liability Insurance for AI

Sharma, A.

Argues that voluntary safety commitments fail under competitive incentives and proposes mandatory third-party liability insurance as financial-responsibility law for high-risk frontier AI development and agentic deployment.

Develops an underwriting framework tying premiums and eligibility to evaluations, red-team remediation, staged release, least-privilege tool access, approval gates, tamper-evident logs, rollback, and incident response.
